Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Overview

Jusstalk does not collect, store, or share personal data. No account is required to use the app.

Data We Process

Data Purpose Third Party Retention
Audio recording Voice-to-text transcription Mistral AI (api.mistral.ai) Deleted immediately after transcription
Transcription text AI formatting (on user request) DeepSeek (api.deepseek.com) Processed in real-time, not stored
Trial usage counter Free trial management None Local device only (UserDefaults), cleared on app uninstall
In-app purchase receipt Purchase verification Apple App Store Handled entirely by Apple StoreKit

Third-Party Services

  • Mistral AI: https://mistral.ai/terms — audio is sent via HTTPS and processed per their API terms
  • DeepSeek: https://platform.deepseek.com — text sent only on explicit user export action
  • Apple App Store: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No name, email, or account data
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No location data
  • No analytics or crash reporting
  • No cookies or tracking
  • No data sold to third parties

Microphone Permission

Jusstalk requests microphone access solely to record audio for transcription. This data is not stored beyond the transcription session.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
