Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start recording?
Tap the microphone button to start, tap again to stop. Your transcription will appear automatically within seconds.
What languages are supported?
Voxtral-Mini primarily supports English and major languages. For the best results, please speak clearly and avoid heavy background noise.
Is my data private?
Yes. Audio is sent to Mistral AI only for transcription and deleted immediately after processing. Text formatting uses DeepSeek, only when you explicitly export. No personal data is stored. Read our full Privacy Policy →
Can I export to PDF?
Yes! We support TXT, Markdown, JSON, PDF, HTML, CSV, and more. You can set your default preference in Settings.
